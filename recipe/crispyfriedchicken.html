Crispy Fried Chicken Recipe

Back to Home

Description

Golden, crispy, and juicy fried chicken with a well-seasoned coating. Perfect as a main dish or with gravy.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken (legs, thighs, or wings)
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch (for extra crispiness)
  • 1 egg (optional, for egg wash)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp paprika (optional, for color)
  • 1 cup buttermilk or milk (for tenderizing, optional)
  • Cooking oil (for deep-frying)

Cooking Steps

  1. Marinate chicken – In a pot, boil beef shank with onions and peppercorns for 1.5–2 hours until tender. Skim off scum.
  2. Prepare coating – Once meat is tender, add corn and potatoes (if using). Simmer 10 mins.
  3. Coat chicken –
    • Dry method: Dredge chicken in flour mixture.
    • Wet method: Dip in beaten egg, then flour mixture (for extra crunch).
  4. Double fry (extra crispy) –
    • First fry: Deep-fry at 325°F (165°C) for 8–10 mins until cooked. Drain.
    • Second fry: Increase heat to 375°F (190°C) and fry again for 2–3 mins until golden brown.
  5. Drain and serve – Place on a wire rack or paper towels. Serve hot with gravy or dipping sauce.

Optional Enhancements:

  • Add a pinch of cayenne pepper for spice, or use a mix of breadcrumbs and flour for texture.
