Kare Kare Recipe

Back to Home

Description

A rich, peanut-based stew with oxtail, tripe, and vegetables (eggplant, string beans, banana blossom), served with bagoong (shrimp paste).

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs oxtail (or tripe, beef, or pork hocks)
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter (or ground roasted peanuts)
  • 1/4 cup annatto seeds (achuete) soaked in 1/2 cup water
  • 1 eggplant, sliced
  • 1 bunch string beans (sitaw), cut
  • 1 banana blossom (puso ng saging), sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups beef or pork broth
  • 2 tbsp rice flour (for thickening)
  • Bagoong (shrimp paste) for serving

Cooking Steps

  1. Boil the meat – Simmer oxtail in water until tender (2–3 hours). Reserve broth.
  2. Sauté aromatics – In a pan, sauté garlic and onions. Add the boiled meat.
  3. Add peanut sauce – Pour broth, peanut butter, and annatto water. Simmer.
  4. Thicken – Mix rice flour with water and stir into the stew.
  5. Add vegetables – Add eggplant, string beans, and banana blossom. Cook until tender.
  6. Serve – Best with bagoong on the side.
