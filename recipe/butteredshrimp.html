Butter Shrimp Recipe

Back to Home

Description

Juicy shrimp cooked in garlicky butter, sometimes with a hint of sweetness from soda or honey.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb large shrimp, cleaned (with or without shell)
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup Sprite/7-Up (optional)
  • 1 tsp honey or sugar (optional)
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Lemon or calamansi juice

Cooking Steps

  1. Sauté garlic – Melt butter in a pan, then sauté garlic until fragrant.
  2. Cook shrimp – Add shrimp and cook until pink (2–3 mins per side).
  3. Add soda/sweetener – Pour Sprite or honey for a slight glaze (optional).
  4. Season – Add salt, pepper, and lemon juice. Serve immediately.
