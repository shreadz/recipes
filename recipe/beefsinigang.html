Beef Sinigang Recipe

Back to Home

Description

A sour and savory Filipino tamarind-based soup with tender beef, vegetables (kangkong, radish, okra), and a tangy broth. Best served hot with steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs beef shank or ribs
  • 1 packet (40g) sinigang mix (tamarind soup base)
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 2 tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 daikon radish (labanos), sliced
  • 6–8 okra, ends trimmed
  • 1 bunch kangkong (water spinach)
  • 2–3 pieces long green chili (siling haba)
  • 4 cups water
  • Fish sauce (patis) or salt to taste

Cooking Steps

  1. Boil the beef – In a pot, boil beef in water with onions and tomatoes until tender (about 1–1.5 hours).
  2. Add sinigang mix – Once beef is tender, add the tamarind soup base and stir.
  3. Add vegetables – Put in radish, okra, and chili. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Add leafy greens – Toss in kangkong and cook for another 2 minutes.
  5. Season – Adjust taste with fish sauce or salt. Serve hot with rice.
